Chocolate Lava Cake

Description

chocolate lava cake is a decadent dessert with a molten chocolate center.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semisweet chocolate
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 6 tbsp all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Butter four 6-oz ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
  3. Melt the butter and chocolate together in a double boiler.
  4. Whisk the powdered sugar, eggs, and egg yolks until light and fluffy.
  5. Fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  6. Gently fold in the flour until just combined.
  7. Divide the batter among the prepared ramekins.
  8. Bake for 12-14 minutes until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  9. Let cool for a minute before inverting onto plates.
