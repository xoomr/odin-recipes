Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad made with fresh romaine lettuce, creamy Caesar dressing, grated Parmesan cheese, and crunchy croutons.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons

steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  3. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the salad.
  4. Add croutons on top and serve immediately.
